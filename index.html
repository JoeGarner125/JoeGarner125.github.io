Project Idea;


  • - Overview;

The project idea I am presenting, is called “Multi-Bank Terminal”.

This is an application, currently in the idea stage, that would be a helpful utility for the everyday businessman, business owners, and would be especially helpful in the Finance industry. The app would be released in Australian only, to begin with. It will incorporate all the major banks’ online banking terminals in one, easy to use master terminal. This would enable people who are with multiple bank accounts with different banks to be able to access all their accounts from the same screen. This application will be made to be compatible for IOS and windows and will be available for Androids, Iphones, tablets, Ipads. It will also be free with in-app purchases.



  • - Motivation;

The finance industry has always been a large interest for me, and this gave me the idea to come up with this project idea. To simplify peoples online banking experience. I personally am a member with 2 separate banks, and it does get annoying having to constantly switch between the two applications to monitor my funds and transfers. Even if you are happy to switch between applications, it can be confusing having to adjust yourself to the layout of a totally different terminal. In our current times, to be successful in anything you do, time and concentration management is very important. If you have all you bank accounts, even if they are with different banks, in the same location using a simple, easy to work with terminal, you will save valuable time, and have a very valuable tool to make you want to do your budgeting. I know if this application was offered to me, I would jump at having an opportunity to simplify my online banking experience. This has the potential to be the next great application in the banking industry. It is a simple concept, but one with great potential.



  • - Description;

Just imagine, it comes to that time of your week when you do your budgeting, either business or personal, and you have the same daunting task ahead of having to jump all over your device to find all the apps and webpages you normally use to get it done. What would you say if there was an application that could potentially save you at least half the time it takes to achieve the very same task? This is exactly what myself and my team would be setting out to do. Our objective with “Multi-Bank Terminal” is to create a free, user friendly application, that allows users to access all their banking details and information from the very same spot. Who doesn’t like making their lives a little simpler, especially when it comes to regular, weekly tasks such as budgeting? 



To log in to Multi-bank Terminal, we will use a multi lock system that has multiple stages of security. A passcode will be generated when first setting up the application, and it will be a randomly generated passcode the site will assign your profile. A number of personal security questions will be provided, and at least 3 must be answered and submitted upon set up of the app. When comes time to log in, the passcode provided to you during set up must be inputted, and 1 of your security questions at random will pop up and must be answered. This provides users with a high level of privacy and security for their banking details. Security would be the most important aspect of developing this application.



In Multi-Bank Terminal’s master terminal (homepage) it will have icons and names for each bank account you have inputted into the terminal, and they will be right in from of you, regardless of which corporation each account is with. There will be a tool bar along the top of the screen with all the different services the app provides. Such as a tutorial on how to get the most out of the terminal and information on what is provided for the users.



Multi-Bank Terminal’s job would primarily be to incorporate all major banks’ online banking terminals but would not be limited to this. We would also integrate with accounting firms around the country, on a commission-based understanding, to have a separate area in the app to offer local financial services anywhere near a desired location. Promotions of firms would be advertised in this section of the application, and there will be a comparison page as well, for users to compare costs, and reviews, to select the financial experts that best suit the job the user wants done. With this tool, online banking and the finance industry will be networked together, for the purpose of saving unnecessary time and effort, with all matters financial.

I would need up to date Android and Apple devices to test the app, even to the extent of tablets and Ipads, to make sure the formatting of the app is compatible with the dimensions of those devices as well.
